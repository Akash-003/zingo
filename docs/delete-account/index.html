Delete Your Account

Last updated: July 8, 2026

You can request deletion of your Zingo account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to submit a request, what gets deleted, and how long it takes.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to akash05singh97@gmail.com with the subject "Zingo Account Deletion Request".
  2. If you signed in with Google, send the request from the same email address you used to sign in, so we can verify account ownership.
  3. We will confirm your request and complete the deletion within 30 days.

Request Account Deletion

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account and sign-in credentials.
  • Your display name and all profile photos (including your Identity Vault).
  • Cards you created.
  • Your push notification token.
  • Usage analytics linked to your user ID.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

What May Be Retained

  • Payment and subscription records, retained as required by law for tax and accounting purposes.
  • Cards you already shared or saved to other apps or devices — these are outside our systems and are not affected.

Guest (Anonymous) Users

If you used Zingo as a guest without signing in, your session is tied to a random anonymous ID on your device. Uninstalling the app ends that session. If you would like the associated server-side data removed as well, contact us at the email above and include the approximate date you used the app; we will do our best to locate and remove it.

Questions

For anything related to your data, see our Privacy Policy or contact us at akash05singh97@gmail.com.
