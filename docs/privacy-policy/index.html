Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Zingo ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile application that lets you personalize and share quote cards with your photo and name. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using Zingo, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1a. Information You Provide

  • Display name: The name you enter during onboarding, shown on cards you create.
  • Profile photos: Photos you upload to personalize your cards. Up to 5 photos can be stored in your Identity Vault.
  • Google account: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google.

1b. Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage analytics: Actions such as viewing cards, sharing, downloading, and changing photos. We record these events to understand how the app is used. No personally identifiable information is attached beyond your anonymous user ID.
  • Push notification token: If you grant notification permission, we store an Expo push token to send you updates.
  • Device information: Standard device identifiers and OS version, collected automatically by the platform.

1c. Guest (Anonymous) Users

You may use Zingo without signing in. In this case, we assign you a random anonymous ID. No name or email is associated with your session unless you later sign in with Google.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize quote cards with your photo and name.
  • To store and sync your profile across devices when you are signed in.
  • To send push notifications (only if you grant permission).
  • To analyze aggregate usage patterns and improve the app.
  • To process subscription payments via Razorpay (for Zingo Premium).

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising profiling.

3. Third-Party Services

Zingo uses the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — Database, authentication, and file storage. Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — Optional authentication. Privacy Policy
  • Remove.bg — Background removal from profile photos. Images are sent to their API and not stored by them beyond processing. Privacy Policy
  • Razorpay — Payment processing for Zingo Premium subscriptions. We do not store your card or payment details. Privacy Policy
  • Expo (Notifications) — Push notification delivery. Privacy Policy

4. Permissions We Request

  • Camera & Photo Library: To let you select or take a profile photo for card personalization. Photos are only uploaded when you explicitly choose them.
  • Notifications: To send you daily card suggestions or reminders. You can disable this at any time in your device settings.
  • Media Library (Save to Device): To save a card image to your camera roll when you tap "Save".

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your profile data, photos, and associated cards are permanently deleted within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

Zingo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for push notifications at any time via device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access-controlled storage via Supabase. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
