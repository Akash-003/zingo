Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Digital Footprints ("Zingo", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, processes, and discloses personal data of users who access or interact with the Zingo platform, including our mobile application.

This Policy is issued in compliance with the Information Technology Act, 2000, and other related and applicable rules and regulations. By using our Platform, you consent to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Display name: The name you enter during onboarding, shown on cards you create and share.
  • Profile photos: Photos you upload to personalise your quote cards. Up to 5 photos can be stored in your Identity Vault.
  • Google account details: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google OAuth.
  • Content you create: Quote cards you publish to the community (if marked public).

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage analytics: In-app actions such as viewing cards, sharing, downloading, changing photos, and selecting categories. These are recorded with an anonymous user ID — not your name or email.
  • Push notification token: If you grant notification permission, we store an Expo push token to deliver updates to your device.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and app crash/diagnostic reports.
  • IP address and general location: Collected automatically for fraud prevention and security purposes.

1.3 Guest (Anonymous) Users

You may use Zingo without signing in. In this case, we assign you a random anonymous ID. No name or email is associated with your session unless you choose to sign in with Google.

1.4 Payment Information

All payment transactions for Zingo Premium are processed by Razorpay, a third-party payment gateway. We do not store your card number, bank details, or any sensitive financial information. Razorpay's handling of payment data is governed by their own privacy policy.

2. Purpose of Collection

  • Account creation, login, and authentication via Google Sign-In.
  • Personalising quote cards with your photo and name.
  • Background removal from profile photos via Remove.bg API for compositing.
  • Delivering and syncing your profile and card collections across sessions.
  • Processing Zingo Premium subscription payments.
  • Sending push notifications (only if you grant permission).
  • Technical troubleshooting, performance analytics, and service improvement.
  • Fraud detection, account security, and abuse prevention.
  • Regulatory compliance and audit requirements.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Consent: When you register and provide your name, photo, or Google account details.
  • Contractual necessity: To deliver subscribed services and features you have requested.
  • Legitimate interest: For platform performance, security monitoring, and abuse prevention.
  • Legal obligation: When required by government or regulatory authorities under applicable law.

4. Disclosure of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data in the following limited circumstances:

  • With third-party service providers under confidentiality agreements, solely to operate the Platform (e.g., Supabase for hosting, Razorpay for payments, Remove.bg for image processing).
  • With government agencies, law enforcement, or regulators upon a lawful request.
  • With successors or acquirers in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of Zingo, our users, or the public.

  • Supabase — Database, authentication, and file storage. Privacy Policy ↗

6. Permissions We Request

  • Camera & Photo Library: To let you select or capture a profile photo for card personalisation. Photos are only uploaded when you explicitly choose them.
  • Push Notifications: To send you daily card suggestions or updates. You can disable this at any time in your device Settings.
  • Media Library (Write): To save a personalised card image to your camera roll when you tap "Save".

7. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored in encrypted databases via Supabase with access control protocols.
  • All data transfers use HTTPS encryption.
  • Role-based access controls restrict who within our team can access user data.
  • Profile photos are stored in a private Supabase Storage bucket accessible only to the account owner.
Despite our safeguards, no system is completely secure. We urge you not to share your account access or any sensitive credentials with anyone.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

  • User data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the service, or as required by applicable law.
  • You may request deletion of your account and data by emailing contact@digitalftprints.com.
  • We will process deletion requests within 30 days, subject to any statutory, legal, security, fraud-prevention, or regulatory obligations that may require limited retention of certain information.

9. User Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or outdated information.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data.
  • Request erasure of your data, subject to legal exemptions.
  • Withdraw notification consent at any time via your device Settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@digitalftprints.com with sufficient identification.

10. Children / Minors

The Platform is not intended for use by individuals below the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a minor without required parental or guardian consent, we will delete such data and suspend the account promptly.

We may modify this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any material changes will be communicated via in-app notifications or email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the Platform after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised Policy.
